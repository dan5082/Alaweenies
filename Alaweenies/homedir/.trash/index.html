Our Story

Meet Abdu

In Libya, there was born a man named Abdu.

Abdu has always had a passion for coffee, but spent the majority of his life teaching and researching databases. Since 2018, Abdu & Co. has been serving hot dogs & coffee to customers all over the United States of America.
